# Legal Differentiation Notes

This project should compete with an app category, not copy a specific app.

## Do not copy

- Brand name, icons, app store copy, or screenshots from another app.
- Proprietary templates, animations, transitions, sounds, or example projects.
- Specific UI layout, navigation, paywall flow, onboarding flow, or pricing language from another app.
- Scraped template packs or media assets.

## Distinct product position

ReelSprint is not a general video editor. It is a local-business promo pack generator.

The narrow job:

> A local business owner enters one offer and gets usable short-form promo concepts, captions, shot lists, and video scene structures.

## Differentiation checklist

- Start with business verticals, not video template browsing.
- Generate campaign packs, not one-off generic templates.
- Focus on local-business CTAs: estimate, reservation, booking, consultation, class sign-up.
- Use a plain operational workflow: offer → hooks → shots → captions → scene spec.
- Build original templates and motion systems from scratch.
- Use original example businesses and no copied sample media.

## Content distribution rule

Do not automate fake engagement or repetitive spam. The launch system should produce 100 distinct posts/month with different hooks, demos, use cases, and founder/build-in-public angles.
