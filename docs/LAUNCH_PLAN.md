# Launch Plan

## Goal

Validate whether local businesses want a narrow promo-pack generator before building a full video editor.

## Audience

Primary:

- Local restaurants
- Realtors
- Home services
- Tutors/classes
- Consultants and professional services

Secondary:

- Med spas/wellness businesses
- Synagogues/community orgs running classes or events
- Small ecommerce shops with local pickup or events

## Offer

Free beta:

> Send one business description and one offer. Get five short-form promo concepts with captions and shot lists.

Paid beta:

> $29 for a done-with-you monthly promo pack.

Software subscription:

- Free: 3 packs/month
- Pro: $14.99/month
- Business: $29.99/month

## 14-day validation sprint

Day 1-2: Build landing page and MVP generator.

Day 3-4: Create 20 demo outputs across five niches.

Day 5-7: DM/email 50 local businesses with a personalized example.

Day 8-10: Post 10 demos showing before/after generation.

Day 11-14: Interview users who clicked, replied, or requested examples.

## Success criteria

- 25+ email/DM replies.
- 10+ businesses request a generated pack.
- 3+ businesses ask for another pack or ask about price.
- 1+ user agrees to pay for monthly packs.

## Build after validation

1. Account system.
2. Saved brand kits.
3. Saved generated packs.
4. Video rendering.
5. Stripe.
6. Self-serve onboarding.
